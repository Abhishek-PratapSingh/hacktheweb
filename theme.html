About Us

This club is established since 2005 and actively running since then , managed beautifully under the Coordinators and Co-Cordinators. Peeps at The Programming Club IIITDMJ aim to help students and as a community we want to grow as much as possible in many domains of coding and development, be it Competitive Programming, Android, Web, Open Source, AI/ML, Ethical Hacking. We try to help each other learn new programming skills organize sessions and competitions let everyone know of ongoing activities in coding community and how they can use them for their benefits.
